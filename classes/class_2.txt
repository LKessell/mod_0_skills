SchoolBus class

Attributes
color (string)
doorOpen (boolean)
numberOfStops (integer)
studentsOnboard (boolean)
pickupLocations (array)

Methods
repaint (changes color)
closeDoor (changes doorOpen to false)
addNewStop (adds 1 to numberOfStops)
dropOff (changes studentsOnboard to false)
changeRoute (modifies pickupLocations array)
